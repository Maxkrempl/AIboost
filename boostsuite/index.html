Frequently Asked Questions

Is BoostSuite free?

Yes! You get 3 free generations per day with no signup. Freelancer (€19/mo) gives you 200/month, and Agency (€49/mo) is unlimited with white-label reports and API access.

Which tools are included?

BoostSuite includes 4 tools: SEO Audit, GEO Check (AI Visibility), Ad Copy Generator, and Listing Optimizer for Etsy/Amazon/Google Business.

What AI models does BoostSuite use?

We use DeepSeek for ad copy and listing optimization, and a combination of web search + DeepSeek for SEO audits and GEO checks.

Can I use BoostSuite for my clients?

Yes! BoostSuite is designed for agencies. You can white-label reports and use the tools for unlimited client projects with a Pro subscription.

Which languages does BoostSuite support?

BoostSuite is available in English, Slovenian, Croatian, Serbian, German, Italian, French, Spanish, Dutch, and Polish.

How does the GEO Check work?

GEO Check searches the web for your business presence across directories, review sites, and social media, then analyzes how likely AI assistants are to recommend you.
